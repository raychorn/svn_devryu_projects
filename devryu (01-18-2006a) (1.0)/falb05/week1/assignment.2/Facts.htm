ScanMaster Scanner

Fact Sheet

Product Summary

Scan your documents with ease and speed

The ScanMaster color/grayscale scanner is an affordable desktop scanner that's quick and easy to use. You can scan color images, grayscale drawings, and text in a single pass, saving you time over multiple-pass desktop scanners. User-friendly software is included so that you can start your scanning in minutes from your applications. Even if you've never used a scanner before, the ScanMaster will have you scanning like a master in minutes.

The ScanMaster is ideal for creating new documents, converting documents, and capturing images and pictures to be used in high-quality presentations, pamphlets, and newsletters. It's ideal for desktop publishing, word processing, and presentation graphics. Use the ScanMaster in conjunction with your faxing and e-mail too.

Best of all, the ScanMaster is backed by Jackson Electronics’ rock-solid reputation for quality and reliability, so you know it's built to last. Plus it comes with a one-year warranty and a responsive service and support plan to keep you up and running.

The ScanMaster scanner is affordable, easy-to-use, and built to last. It is your home office scanning solution.

Features

  • A complete, affordable solution to all your color and grayscale image and text scanning needs.
  • 1200-dpi enhanced resolution and 300-dpi optical resolution ensure quality output to your desktop printer.
  • Single-pass scanning provides fast output and quality scans of color and grayscale images.
  • Use the enclosed ImageScan software to automatically scan images into your documents.
  • Photographs can be scanned in 256 levels of grays and over 16.7 million colors.
  • Increased productivity through automated features and a simplified user interface. Output is automatically optimized for a broad range of printers and output devices.
  • Easily scan text using Jackson Electronics' WordScan technology and integrated OCR software.
  • TWAIN/OLE support lets you scan pictures, logos, drawings and other images directly into your application.
  • Use ScanFile software to quickly find and organize scanned images and electronic documents visually instead of by filename, and provide automated links to a variety of applications, such as e-mail, e-fax, and word processing.
  • Scan different types of media, including simple photographs, office documents, books, and magazines.
  • Scans pages up to 8.5 x 14 inches.
  • Supports the Windows and Macintosh operating systems.
  • Optional 50-page automatic document feeder for hands-free scanning.
