Frequently Asked Questions

How do I scan slides?

You can scan slides using the JE Transparency Adapter (part number STA8901), available from Jackson Electronics.

Where can I find the latest drivers for my ScanMaster?

You can download the latest software drivers from the Jackson Electronics Web page.

What is the largest sheet that I can scan?

8.5 x 14 in (216 x 356 mm).
